Steps of Online Application


1.lease select the academic year


2.Enter Mobile No. to receive Password which will be reflected on the screen.


3.Please note the further communication will be done in this mobile number only through normal message.


4.Select class for admission


5.Fill-up the Pre-Application form & Apply


6.Wait for the approval of Pre-Application form by the School Authorities


7.If Approved the payment link will be sent via Email/SMS/Call


8.Click on the payment link & choose your payment option to make the payment (Prospectus Fee + Application Fee)


9.Once the payment is registered with us, please wait for the message regarding admit card and interaction/assessment date from the school authorities via Email/SMS.


10.In case of payments debited from your account and Registration No. not issued, in such case please wait for 48 hours and then contact the school office at info@dpskolkata.com


11.Incase of payments made offline, collect your Application form from School Admissions Office


12.Fill-up the Application form and Apply


13.Take the print-out of the applied Application form


14.Paste the photographs and carry the Application form during the assessment/observation/interaction/Interview along with signatures and necessary documents as per the required guidelines and instructions
