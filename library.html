LIBRARY RULES

Please follow the guidelines:

Rule 1:Please avoid making loud noises in the library.

Rule 2:Please keep any device with you in silent.

Rule 3:Please do not litter.

Rule 4:Please do not tear pages off the books.

Rule 5:Please keep the books clean and in a reading condition

Rule 6:Please make sure that you put the books back at the right shelf and category.

Rule 7:Please do not borrow any book without informing the librarian.

Rule 8:Please return the books that you have borrowed on time
